Your First Pet Dragon

Dragons make rewarding pets, though they do require a bit of effort. They have been known to set valuable items on fire if they feel neglected, so make sure that you have the time to spend with them before you get your first dragon.

Some items you will require before you bring your dragon home are:

  1. a suitable nest area
  2. food
  3. fresh running water
  4. toys
  5. access to the sky

Some ideas for toys for your new dragon are:

  • RC plane/helicopter for your dragon to chase in the air
  • laser pointer for your dragon to chase on land
  • straw piles for your dragon to set on fire

Remember, the most important thing is to love your dragon and give him/her the attention and supplies they need to be happy and thrive.
